About Us

Herat Saffron LLC is the United States branch of Afghanistan Saffron Company which was established in 2003 and recognized as “Afghanistan Best Exporter” by the Afghanistan Chamber of Commerce and Industries (ACCI) every year since 2012. Growing wheat, fruits, and seeds as well as saffron on around 800 hectares of land, it is one of Afghanistan’s largest agribusinesses. Renowned for competitive prices and fast, reliable delivery, Afghanistan Saffron Company markets top-quality saffron to wholesale and retail clients worldwide, with Herat Saffron™ as its premium saffron brand. The company also offers private label packaging solutions for retailers who wish to market high-quality, own-brand Afghan saffron. All products are GFSI recognized based on FSSC22000, ISO 22000 and HACCP certified.
